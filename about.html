About the Author

Aaron Rainey is currently a Senior at the Denver School of Science and Technology at Stapleton, and is in the class of 2018. Aaron Rainey enjoys all of his classes encluding his computer programing class taught by Mr. Brock Hoover.When Aaron is not at school, with family, or friends, he is in the Civil Air Patrol. Aaron is apart of the Valkyrie Cadet Squadron (RMR-183), which is apart of the Colorado Wing, and within the National Command of Civil Air Patrol. Aaron has achieved the rank Cadet Second Lieutenant (C/2nd Lt), and is currently the Alpha Flight Commander.
